Blue Hen Bowl

Description

KFC Who???

Ingredients

  • Chicken Nuggets
  • Instant Mashed Potatoes
  • Corn, Frozen

Steps

  1. Make the Nuggs to manufacturer Directions in air fryer
  2. Make the instant mash potatoes. Follow Directions on box.
  3. Microwave the frozen corn
  4. Mix it all up and eat it!
