Fried Rice

Description

You Tellin Me A Shrimp Fried This Jawn?

Ingredients

  • Rice, Cooked
  • Bacon
  • Egg

You can put whatever else you want in there too. I like a lil soy sauce and hot sauce.

Steps

  1. Fry up the Bacon
  2. Crush it into itty bits
  3. Mix it in with the rice
  4. In the bacon pan, toss the rice in
  5. As the rice fries, mix in the egg
  6. Add hot sauce after cooking if you want
